I'll give your brand a depth

Hi, there! I’m Kristina Nazarevska, creative designer originally from Kyiv, Ukraine. Currently in Warsaw, Poland. I’ve had over 10 years of graphic design experience, working with startups, well-known companies and international clients from concept to final deliverables. In recent years I was not satisfied with just graphic design, so I’ve found a passion in 3d, UI and motion design - it helps a lot to breathe life into the brand. My work approach is individual and complex - whether it’s identity, advertising, website - I will reveal the brand in the best way. There is rather big journey of your own brand formation and I love the way of process when the brand blossoms from concept/strong idea to full visual communication and implementation.
